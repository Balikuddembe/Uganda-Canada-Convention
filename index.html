UGANDA CANADA CONVENTION 2023

Don’t Miss Canada Day | The Uganda-
Canadian Business Expo and Convention

The Uganda Canada Convention, Festival & Investment Summit 2023 edition will be held at the
Westin Hotel,Ottawa – Canada from Friday June 30th to July 2nd, Canada Day Weekend.

Highlights will a B2B Trade and Investment Summit, a Cultural Festival and a Convention Gala dinner.

Other highlights include a National Prayer Service, Sunset Boat Cruise and a Grand Finale Uganda Night Celebration.
